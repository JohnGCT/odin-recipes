Carrot Fusilli

A casserole pasta dish that is easy to make and filling. One can use other pastas than fusilli

Ingredients

  • 500g of pasta
  • 1 tables spoon of coarse salt
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 tablespoons of tomatoe sauce
  • 3 mashed cooked carrots
  • 500g of grated calabria sausage
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of requeijão
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • salt and black pepper
  • grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put water on a pot to boil it, while you prepare and cook the ingredients
  2. Add the butter to a pot on low heat and let it melt. Add the onions and fry them until golden brown
  3. Add the carrots and fry until cooked
  4. Add the mayonnaise and the requeijão. Stir until homogenous. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Cook it until it starts to boil and let it rest
  6. When the water is boiling, add the salt and the pasta. Let it cook to your preference
  7. Drain the pasta when ready and let it rest.
  8. On a glass casserole add the pasta and the tomato sauce. Mix them well
  9. Add the grated sausage on top, forming a layer
  10. Add the carrot sauce on top of the sausage, forming a layer
  11. Add the grated parmesan cheese on top of the sauce
  12. Preheat the oven on high heat for 10min
  13. Lower the heat to 210C and put the casserole in
  14. Let it cook until the tomato sauce is boiling and the sauce is lightly browned
  15. Grill the top of the casserole if possible
  16. When done let it cool down for 5 min and serve it hot
